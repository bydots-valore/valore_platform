About Bydots

Making expert knowledge operational.

Bydots builds VALORE — a governed system for capturing what experts know and putting it to work, at scale, for humans and AI alike.

Why we built this.

Every organisation has knowledge that never gets documented — the judgement calls, the hard-won lessons, the standards that live in people's heads. When those people leave, that knowledge goes with them. When those people are overwhelmed, the quality gap shows.

VALORE exists to change that. Not by automating away expertise, but by making it reproducible — encoded, governed, and improving every time it runs.

"The problem is not that people lack knowledge. The problem is that knowledge is not yet infrastructure."

What we do

We build governed knowledge infrastructure.

Elicit

We surface the tacit expertise that lives in people's heads — through conversation, document analysis, live work and more.

Encode

We make that expertise reproducible — structured, governed, and auditable in a way that scales without losing fidelity.

Govern

We keep every output accountable to the encoded expertise — for humans and AI agents alike, every time it runs.

VALORE Platform

One system, coordinated across Suites, Apps and Agents.

VALORE is not a single tool — it is a coordinated system. Suites handle whole classes of knowledge work. Apps give people direct access. Agents carry out governed tasks.
