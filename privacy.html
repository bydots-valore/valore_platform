1. Introduction

Bydots Pty Ltd ('Bydots', 'we', 'us', 'our') is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it. It applies to information collected through our website (bydots.com), our platform (VALORE), and any other interactions with Bydots.

2. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly to us and information generated through your use of our services. This includes:

  • Contact information — name, email address, phone number, organisation name
  • Usage information — how you interact with our website and platform
  • Inquiry content — the content of messages or forms you submit to us
  • Technical information — browser type, IP address, referring URL (collected automatically)

3. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to operate and improve our services. Specifically:

  • To respond to inquiries and provide requested information
  • To operate and improve the VALORE platform for clients
  • To send relevant communications (with your consent)
  • To comply with legal obligations
We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data storage and security

Your information is stored securely. We apply industry-standard security measures to protect it from unauthorised access. Bydots is based in Australia and data is stored in accordance with Australian law.

5. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies for essential functionality. We do not use third-party advertising cookies. You may disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting your ability to use the site.

6. Third parties

We do not disclose your personal information to third parties except as required to operate our services (e.g. hosting providers) or where required by law.

7. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct or delete your personal information held by us. To exercise these rights, contact us at solutions@bydots.com.
